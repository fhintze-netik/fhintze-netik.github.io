About Me

I am a software development apprentice with a great passion for programming, video games and motorcycles. I'm getting trained in developing efficient and user-friendly solutions and have experience in C#, AL, HTML, CSS, object-oriented design, and a little bit of JavaScript. I enjoy taking on complex challenges and developing innovative applications that improve the user experience. I'm keen to expand my knowledge and learn about new topics or technologies and apply them to develop good, robust solutions that meet both technical requirements and user preferences.

Frontend Development

In the course of this portfolio and my other training, I have already learned a lot about the front end. Through various projects and tasks I was able to further my education and I always enjoy writing frontend-related lines.

Backend Development

However, the most exciting area of development for me remains in the backend. This is where I've had the most to do so far. I'm still at the beginning of my training and can't keep up with experience yet, but I make up for that with motivation.

Projects

Card Game

One of the tasks I was given as part of my training was to develop a card game. The aim of the game was to collect as many sets of four matching cards as possible. I worked through the entire process of programming the game, from the planning phase to the final implementation. For this project, I specifically used C# and utilized its object-oriented functions to manage the logic and components of the game. It was really fun to program.
